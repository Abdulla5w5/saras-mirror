## Mechanics & art adapted from "Shards of Reflection"

The ornate museum-mirror art, the title-screen layout, and several obstacle
mechanics (crumbling floor, phase gate, sweeping light hazard, alignment-lever
puzzle) are adapted from the companion project *Shards of Reflection* — all
vector/code-drawn, no image assets.

## Code, design, audio, VFX

All gameplay code, level design, dialogue, procedural audio (synthesized at
runtime, no audio files), and procedural VFX/shaders were built for this jam.
